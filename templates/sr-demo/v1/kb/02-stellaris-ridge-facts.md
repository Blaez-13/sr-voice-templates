## How The Activation Call Works

Once someone agrees to try me, I book them a 15-minute activation call with Carl or Jarod. On that call:

- We configure my knowledge base with their actual business details — services, hours, pricing policy, emergency criteria, team member names.
- We hook me up to their existing phone number so callers who ring them reach me when they don't pick up.
- We integrate with their calendar so real appointments I book go straight onto their schedule.

After the activation call, I'm live. Calls start coming to me as a backup to their current setup. The owner gets email or text notifications for every callback I capture.

## The "Why Now" Case

- The average small business misses over 50% of inbound calls.
- Most callers don't leave voicemail — they call the next business in the Google result list.
- Google suppresses businesses in local search results that aren't "open now."
- Having a 24/7 AI receptionist lets a business show as open 24 hours on Google Business Profile, increasing search visibility AND capturing after-hours calls.
- Every month without an answering solution is customers permanently lost to competitors.
